About me

I am a fifth year Ph.D student at Cornell University in the Department of Computer Science, advised by Prof. Andrew Myers.

Prior to joining Cornell, I received a Bachelor's in Computer Science from ACM Honors Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.

My research interests are broadly in computer security, systems and programming language. And I focuses on designing and buliding secure systems.
